Why EdgarFlash

  
EDGAR publishes thousands of filings per day. By the time you find the relevant one in your normal flow, the market has already moved. EdgarFlash watches the firehose for you, filters by what you care about, and delivers within seconds. Built on Cloudflare's edge — runs in 200+ locations, polls every minute (cron minimum on Workers), pushes through to your endpoint with sub-100ms transit.






  
API

  
GET /api/feed — delayed public feed; real-time with Authorization: Bearer ef_live_...

  
GET /api/realtime — paid API-key real-time feed

  
POST /api/subscribe — register (body: {email, webhook_url, plan, forms?, tickers?})

  
POST /api/confirm — confirm payment and receive your API key (body: {quote_id, tx_hash})

  
GET /api/status — system health

  
GET /api/subscription/<id> — your subscription status
